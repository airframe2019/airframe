EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

David Damamme
Vice-President Aerospace Industry ALTRAN


Airbus Airframe is living an exciting transformation moment. Group's main objectives are strongly echoing in your activities. The Single Aisle production ramp up impacts your design process and its economical optimization creates a need of design to value redevelopment. On top, the anticipation of new PDP and the DDMS impact will dramatically change your everyday work.  

Altran will be able to support these objectives relying on two axis: 

  • OPERATIONAL EXCELLENCE: Altran will bring, maintain and increase their technical assets and capabilities to carry out the scope with a proven quality level. Try it now!
  • TRANSFORMATION: Altran will bring their capabilities based on three pillars: Try it now! Try it now!

DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION

We will rely on our assests (250 data scientists in Tessella or more than 8000 engineers in digital transformation from the former Aricent) combined with its strong knowledge of production skills and methods.

TRANSNATIONAL HARMONIZATION

Altran will manage projects with a transnational aggregated view on 5 natcos (France, Germany, UK, Spain and India). Our geographical implementation will allow us to answer to the « follow the sun » requirements.

BEST PRICE COUNTRIES

Altran is supported by efficient and proactive Engineering Centers based in India, Morocco & Mexico.

This combination will allow us to continue enhancing our way of working on non specific design work activities (Methods & Services and Material & Processes), enlarge our offer in support activities (PET/MAP and Customer Support) and support Airbus in the transformation of the specific design work activities (ESI, MSI and Structure).

On this last point, Altran is currently sharing the same interest: Transform the way of working, to go faster while reducing the workloads. Together, we have a suitable size allowing us to cover all skills and to ensure a good delivery.

Our alliance is highly motivated for supporting Airbus in achieving its objectives in daily delivery excellence and deep transformation.
